Core Ideas

Two concepts, one insight

Small reasoning errors don't stay small. They amplify through every layer of a decision chain — in supply chains, in teams, in AI.

Cognitive Bullwhip Effect

In supply chain, the Bullwhip Effect turns a 5% demand dip into a 40% production cut. Each node amplifies the original distortion.

AI reasoning does the same thing. A small misread compounds through framing, analysis, and recommendation — producing a confidently wrong output from a minor input error.

→ NPS drops 8 points
→ No root cause check
→ "Redesign onboarding"
→ 3 months wasted dev resources
→ 8-point signal. 6-figure cost.

Structured Cognition Model

Most diagnostic tools measure what you know. This one measures how you think — the structural quality of reasoning itself.

The model defines six levels of cognitive maturity. At Level 1, you react to symptoms. At Level 6, you design the system that prevents symptoms from occurring. The gap between each level isn't knowledge — it's the ability to see one layer deeper into causation.
